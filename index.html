The Cargo Sorting DNA

Welcome

Since the advent of DNA nanotechnology, scientists have been pushing the limits of what we can achieve at the nanoscale. The use of DNA, given its well-known programmability and physical properties, has enabled us to pursue the creation of complex nanomachines. DNA robots are one such machine, with the potential to carry out highly specific tasks. Our team, the Cargo Code-Breakers, has chosen to pursue the development of these robots. Previous iterations of DNA robots have been designed with more limited functionality in terms of their movement and ability to carry out tasks. Our model has been developed with the aim of performing cargo sorting along with extended movement capabilities. We believe that the continued development of these nanomachines may one day have use in targeted drug delivery.
